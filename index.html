About Vispi

Ph.D. Student • Northwestern University

Projects Education Publications Conference Papers

As a Ph.D. student at Northwestern University, my focus is centered on the development and implementation of machine learning algorithms for Digital Twins. I'm deeply immersed in projects encompassing time-series analysis, language models, deep learning, uncertainty quantification, and optimization. My proficiency lies in creating ML models for time-series analysis and building optimization algorithms for real-time decision-making.

My research is oriented toward constructing digital twin systems—digital replicas of physical systems that learn from their real-world counterparts in real time. These virtual models enable instantaneous decisions by providing actionable feedback to the physical system, harmonizing the physical and digital to drive robust, data-driven operations.
