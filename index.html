Research Focus

I develop machine learning algorithms for Digital Twins—virtual replicas that learn from physical systems in real time to enable fast, data‑driven decisions. My work spans time‑series analysis, large language models, deep learning, uncertainty quantification, and optimization, with an emphasis on deployable methods that close the loop between prediction and control.
