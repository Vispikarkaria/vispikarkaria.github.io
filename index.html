Research Focus

As a Ph.D. student at Northwestern University, my work centers on developing machine learning algorithms for Digital Twins — spanning time-series analysis, language models, deep learning, uncertainty quantification, and optimization. I build time-series ML models and optimization algorithms for real-time decision-making.

A digital twin is a digital replica of a physical system that learns from its real-world counterpart in real time, enabling instantaneous, data-driven actions. My research advances this harmony between physical and digital systems.
