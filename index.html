As a Ph.D. student at Northwestern University, my focus is centered on the development and implementation of machine learning algorithms for Digital Twins. I'm immersed in time-series analysis, language models, deep learning, uncertainty quantification, and optimization—building models tailored for time-series and algorithms for real-time decision making.

My research aims to create digital replicas of physical systems that learn from real-world counterparts in real time and provide actionable feedback—making decisions instantaneously and robustly.
