About Vispi

PhD @ Northwestern ML & Digital Twins UQ Optimization
Projects Publications

I am a Ph.D. student at Northwestern University focused on designing and deploying machine learning algorithms for Digital Twins. My work spans time-series modeling, language models, deep learning, uncertainty quantification, and decision optimization.

I build models and optimization pipelines that connect physical systems to their digital counterparts in real time— enabling actionable feedback, robust predictions, and smarter control. The goal is harmonious, closed-loop intelligence between the twin and the real world.
