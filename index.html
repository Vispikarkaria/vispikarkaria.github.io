About Vispi

Ph.D. student at Northwestern University building ML systems for Digital Twins and real-time decision-making.

Projects Education Publications Conference Papers

Research Focus

I design and deploy machine learning algorithms for Digital Twins: time-series models, language-model tooling, deep learning, uncertainty quantification, and optimization for real-time decisions. My work builds virtual replicas that continuously learn from physical systems and provide actionable feedback to close the loop between model and reality.
