About Vispi

Projects Education Publications Conference Papers

As a Ph.D. student at Northwestern University, my focus is centered on the development and implementation of machine learning algorithms for Digital Twins. I'm immersed in projects spanning time-series analysis, language models, deep learning, uncertainty quantification, and optimization—designing models for time-series and optimization algorithms for real-time decision-making.

My research builds digital twin systems—learning from physical counterparts in real time to enable instantaneous decisions and actionable feedback. The harmony between physical and digital systems drives my work.
